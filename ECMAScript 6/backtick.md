# BACKTICK
 penggunaan ```backtick`` sangat peraktis dan mudah di gunakan 
 
 #### contoh 1
 ```javascript
 let nilaiA = 5;
let nilaiB = 3;
let nilaiAB = `nilai A adalah ${nilaiA} nilai B adalah ${nilaiB}`;
console.log(nilaiAB);
 ```

 #### contoh 2
 ```javascript
 const paragraph = `
I am a new paragraph.
And, you can make a new 'break line' inside of me.
`;

console.log(paragraph);
 ```
